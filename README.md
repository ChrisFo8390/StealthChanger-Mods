# Stealthchanger Mods and qol updates

In process of upgrading my 300mm V2.4 with extra z-travel into a Stealthchanger, I got through many iterations.<br/>
Some parts are slightly modified from [StealthChanger by DraftshiftDesign](https://github.com/DraftShift/StealthChanger), others are made by myself.

### [A4T Docks](https://github.com/ChrisFo8390/StealthChanger-Mods/tree/main/A4T%20Docks)
These are made from scratch and adress the problem with docking A4T due to special form of fan ducts.

### [Cartographer mount for Fysetc SC Shuttle](https://github.com/ChrisFo8390/StealthChanger-Mods/tree/main/CNC%20Shuttle%20Carto%20Mount)
Mount for Cartographer (maybe Beacon - untested) on CNC Shuttle with umbilicial.

### [CableManagement CNLinko with PTFE Pushfitting](https://github.com/ChrisFo8390/StealthChanger-Mods/tree/main/CableManagement)
Loved the CableManagement Mod from [N3MI-DG](https://github.com/DraftShift/CableManagement/tree/main/UserMods/N3MI-DG/Umbilical_plates_V2)
but wanted to make the tools detachable without demounting parts in the back - CNLinko connectors aren't cheap, only 5 tools version.

### [Hexa Distro Fusion Backpack](https://github.com/ChrisFo8390/StealthChanger-Mods/tree/main/Hexa%20Distro%20Fusion%20backpack)
Backpack plus cableduct modded from the very nice Design of [Drakarah](https://www.printables.com/model/1366367-fysetc-hexa-distro-fusion-backpack) made it a bit more rigid and two color optinal.
